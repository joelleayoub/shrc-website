About SHRC

A Legacy of Rehabilitation in Lebanon

Founded in 1990 by Dr. Mohamad Halimi, the Saad Halimi Rehabilitation Center has served patients across Tripoli and North Lebanon for over three decades.

Our purpose is to help you continue participating in your daily activities and life roles. We design treatment programs based on both the findings of our evaluation and your personal goals — because movement and function matter to every aspect of your life.

We provide therapeutic treatment wherever movement and function are threatened by aging, injury, disease, or environmental factors, with one goal: improving your quality of life.

SHRC is fully equipped with state-of-the-art rehabilitation and preventive technology, combining Western evidence-based medicine with effective Eastern therapeutic traditions for a truly integrative approach to care.

Our Specialized Services

Each service is delivered by trained specialists and tailored to your condition and recovery goals.

01
Isokinetic Therapy
Using the HUMAC® NORM™ system, we quantify muscular deficits before and after treatment — designing precise rehabilitation programs and objectively measuring your recovery progress.
Learn more
02
Acupuncture
Drawing from Chinese and Japanese traditions, our acupuncture releases active and latent trigger points, reduces pain pathways, and restores structural balance in the body.
Learn more
03
Osteopathy
Treating the body holistically — addressing spinal blockages, pelvic torsion, and musculoskeletal dysfunction to relieve back pain, headaches, digestive issues, and dizziness.
Learn more
04
Medical Thermography
Infrared imaging that visualizes pain and disease quickly and non-invasively. A powerful diagnostic tool that identifies the root cause of your symptoms before treatment begins.
Learn more
05
Ergonomy
We evaluate your workplace, sleep habits, and daily activities to identify risk factors and recommend ergonomic solutions — helping you prevent musculoskeletal problems before they develop.
Learn more
06
Women's Health
Specialized pelvic rehabilitation for urinary incontinence, uterine prolapse, dyspareunia, vaginismus, and menstruation-related back pain — in a confidential, caring environment.
Learn more
07
SkinPen Microneedling
The first FDA-cleared microneedling device. Clinically proven to improve acne scars and wrinkles with minimal downtime and results lasting up to six months.
Learn more
08
Cupping Therapy
Reduces inflammation and muscle tension, enhances blood circulation, stimulates the immune system, and provides effective relief for back pain and migraines.
