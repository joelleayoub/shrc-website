About SHRC

A Legacy of Rehabilitation in Lebanon

Founded in 1990 by Dr. Mohamad Halimi, the Saad Halimi Rehabilitation Center has served patients across Tripoli and North Lebanon for over three decades.

Our purpose is to help you continue participating in your daily activities and life roles. We design treatment programs based on your evaluation findings and personal goals, because movement and function matter to every aspect of your life.

SHRC is fully equipped with state-of-the-art rehabilitation technology, combining Western evidence-based medicine with effective Eastern therapeutic traditions for a truly integrative approach to care.

Our Specialized Services

Each service is delivered by trained specialists and tailored to your condition and recovery goals.

01
Isokinetic Therapy
HUMAC NORM technology for precise muscle testing and rehabilitation, measuring your progress objectively before and after treatment.

02
Acupuncture
Chinese and Japanese acupuncture techniques to release trigger points, reduce pain, and restore structural balance throughout the body.
Learn more
03
Osteopathy
Holistic treatment addressing spinal blockages, pelvic torsion, and musculoskeletal dysfunction to relieve pain and restore mobility.
Learn more
04
Medical Thermography
Infrared imaging that visualizes pain and disease non-invasively, identifying the root cause of symptoms before treatment begins.
Learn more
05
Ergonomy
Workplace and lifestyle assessment to identify risk factors and recommend ergonomic solutions, preventing problems before they develop.
Learn more
06
Women's Health
Specialized pelvic rehabilitation for urinary incontinence, uterine prolapse, dyspareunia, and menstruation-related back pain.
