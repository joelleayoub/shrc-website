Case Study · Isokinetic Therapy · December 2022
How a 16-Year-Old Professional Tennis Player Gained 26% Muscle Strength in 4 Sessions
Age 16, Male 185 cm, 72 kg 4 daily sessions Tripoli, Lebanon

The patient is a competitive tennis player actively participating in tournaments in Lebanon. He was referred to SHRC for isokinetic muscle conditioning of the quadriceps, the primary muscles responsible for explosive movement, change of direction, and serve mechanics in tennis.

Treatment used Isogei muscle stimulation technology on both thighs over four consecutive daily sessions. Two channels were applied per thigh: one targeting the rectus femoris, and a second targeting the sartorius and vastus medialis. To address the natural muscular asymmetry common in tennis players, the intensity on the dominant right side was set 10% higher than the left.

Following the treatment course, the patient was assessed on the HUMAC NORM Isokinetic Testing System, the gold standard in objective muscle performance measurement.

"We have been using Vip Italia machines since 2005 and have consistently received outstanding results with our patients."
Dr. Saad M. Halimi, DPT, Ms.Ost, L.Ac, Director of SHRC
